Poster Submission Guidelines

Format & Size

  • Size: A0 portrait (841 Ã— 1189 mm)
  • Resolution: Minimum 300 DPI
  • Format: PDF or high-quality print

Content Requirements

  • Clear, readable fonts (minimum 24pt for body text)
  • Title should be visible from 2 meters away
  • Include author names and affiliations
  • Abstract, methodology, results, and conclusions
  • References in standard academic format
