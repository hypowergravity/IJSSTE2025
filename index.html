About IJSSTE Hokkaido Symposium 2025

The IJSSTE Hokkaido Symposium 2025 focuses on the crucial collaboration between different fields of sciences and technology including experimental, computational, cheminformatics, AI, and more to design new chemicals, materials, and biomaterials.

A major objective of the symposium is to create an opportunity for students and scientists in Japan to showcase their research, exchange ideas, and foster collaborations across disciplines.

Multidisciplinary Approach

Bringing together experimental, computational, cheminformatics, and AI expertise
